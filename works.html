Why is it even important to visualise an idea and what does it mean? Firstly, there are 2 main phases where you will need a visualisation. Initially for yourself. Secondly, to communicate your idea to investors, partners or customers. In this talk I will share some techniques, which we designers use when creating our works. They will help you to better understand such crucial points as who your target audience is and what these peopleâ€™s needs and expectations are. These techniques will present your idea under difference angles and shape it to become easy-to-grasp.
